Max Headroom

WebGL Cyberpunk Background

The authentic Max Headroom background - a simple rotating 3D cube with bright neon colors. Each face displays a different vibrant 80s color (cyan, magenta, yellow, green, red, blue) as it spins on random axes, positioned close enough that you only see one face at a time.

• Authentic rotating cube animation

• Bright neon 80s color palette

• Controllable speed: speed="0.1" to "2.0"

• Built as a reusable web component
